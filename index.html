Who We Are

Led by Morgan Kelly Baird

MKBeauty Artistry began with one artist and grew into a curated team delivering an elevated standard of bridal beauty across the Southeast and into Upstate New York. Every artist on our roster is selected and trained to the same standard, so wherever you find us, the experience feels the same: considered, calm, and entirely about you.

We work alongside brides, their mothers, their bridal parties, and everyone gathered to celebrate. The same care goes into a quiet morning in a getting ready suite as a full wedding weekend with forty guests in tow.
