What Is Always Included

A few things that come with every service

Lashes

Strip or individual lashes are included with every makeup service at no extra charge, across all tiers.

Previews

A preview session is your chance to design your look before the day arrives. Previews are priced at your bridal service rate and paid at the time of service, separate from your wedding day balance.

A Prep Guide

Every client receives a full event prep guide with their contract, covering everything you need to know in the weeks and days leading up to your wedding.

Touch-up attendance is available exclusively for Cachemire tier brides as an optional add-on, always quoted and itemized separately. It is never included in base pricing.

Travel

We will go wherever your day takes you.

Whether your wedding is in the Tennessee mountains, along the Carolina coast, the Finger Lakes, or somewhere we have never been before, we will find a way to be there. We genuinely love destination work.

Travel fees are quoted per event based on distance, logistics, and scheduling. For events requiring flights, lodging, or extended travel, all of that is outlined clearly in your quote before you commit to anything. Nothing is a surprise.

Reach out and we will put together something that works.

Payment Schedule

Simple, straightforward payments

01

Booking Fee

Due at signing. This is what holds your date and removes it from availability. Non-refundable, and the amount is outlined in your custom quote.

02

First Installment

Fifty percent of your remaining balance is due 60 days prior to your event. Your exact due date is calculated and listed in your quote.

03

Final Balance

The remaining balance is due 10 days before your wedding day. Again, your specific date will be in your quote so there is nothing to calculate on your end.

Preview sessions are paid separately at the time of service and are not part of the above schedule. For bookings made within 7 days of the event date, full payment is due at the time of booking, no exceptions. This applies to previews as well. Accepted payment methods include Zelle, Venmo, cash, and credit card.
